Why a pet sitter?

  • Your pet will be safe and comfortable in her own home.
  • Your pet's own routine can be followed.
  • We will give your pet his special treats.
  • We can administer medication.
  • Loving attention is our specialty!
  • You will have a much more relaxing vacation or business trip knowing your pet is getting personal attention.
  • With our attention to home security details, your home will not look vacant.
  • If you are working long hours, we can walk or play with your pet on a regular basis.

About us

Dotty

After being a psychotherapist in private practice for 16 years, Dotty Armstrong, founder of Dotty's Pet Sitting Svc, decided to give in to her life-long love of animals. Since 1992 Dotty and the pet sitters she hand-picked to work with her over the years have always had one goal, that is to make sure your pets are happy and safe.

Diane

PetSitter/Owner/Coordinator

I joined Dotty's in 2006. Although I was trained as a marine biologist, I was drawn to a number of other occupations as well. However, my passion has always been caring for animals of all kinds. It may be hard to hug a fish, but it was very easy for me to say "yes" in 2013 when Dotty asked me to be her manager. Dotty has since retired, left the business and is now happily spending more time with her own pets. However, we sitters are still here keeping Dotty's values, love of animals and caring style intact! When you contact us I will match you with the right pet sitter for your particular pets.

Services

Each custimized visit includes:

  • Pampered attention, we will pet, brush and talk to your pet.
  • Replenish food and water each visit (including your pet's special treats).
  • Play, exercise and/or walk your pet.
  • Administer medication, if needed.
  • Pet waste clean-up (including litter box).
  • Plants watered.
  • Home safety detail: bring in mail, newspapers, close/open drapes, turn lights on/off, put garbage and recycling out.
